1.000 Pictures in a Day is a project that I have been considering for the past year. Every photo listed was taken on a 35mm film camera, either with the Canon SureShot or Olympus Infinity, on March 30 to March 31st.

Nothing inspired me to do this project and this project serves no purpose other than to exist.
